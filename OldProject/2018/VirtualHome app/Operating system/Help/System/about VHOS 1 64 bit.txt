About VirtualHome OS 1.00 64 bit

Description: Virtualize your property! Want to give a house tour WITHOUT leaving your house? You now can! With virtualhome.
Emulate your house/business structure, and share it over the server console. You can then let people from all around the world
come to your house virtually! You can also record their activities, and upload them.
This program can be ran on a virtual machine, however, one system covers only 1 room. You can use multiple devices to simulate your entire property
